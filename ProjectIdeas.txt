==Project Ideas==

Web app that generates a new 'challenge' out of submitted challenges to present to users daily (or on request).
Logins via google for ez login.

Python-Django based restful webapp that generates the daily challenge and also runs a neural network daily on submitted ideas to filter for top ones based on common keywords. ie; Of all submitted articles in the last 6 months filter for top keywords.
For all submitted ideas today, apply nueral network filtering for those same keywords to present ideas, then randomise.

Laravel PHP for the front end, website part of it.
